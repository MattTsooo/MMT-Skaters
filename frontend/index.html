About Us

We’re Melody and Megan — two sisters who fell in love with ice skating at just four years old. From our first spins on the local rink to years of competitive skating, we’ve grown up with ice under our feet and passion in our hearts. Today, we’re proud to share that passion by coaching skaters of all ages — from first-timers to aspiring performers.

Our lessons are fun, personalized, and designed to build both skill and confidence. Whether you’re dreaming of graceful spins or just want to stay on your feet — we’ll help you find your rhythm on the ice!
